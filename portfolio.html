Freeway, Digital Video, 2019

The digital video combines my own recorded video as well as my own recorded sound. Driving down the freeway in the evening often puts me into a daze as I see the sun setting. The ticking represents the feeling of the drive being soporific and dull.

Self-Reflection, Digital Image, 2019

This is a digital composite image using various images I found online. The marble statues, which remind me of weeping angels, depicts the unsettling feeling I get when looking into the bathroom mirror alone. The water on the floor could be from the overflowing sinks or perhaps tears.

Pokemon Battle, Digital Image, 2019

This is a digital composite image using a scenic forest image and pokemon pixel sprites. As a kid, I often dreamed about what it would be like if pokemon existed in the real world but when I imagine this - I am often in the same world shown in the Pokemon show. I think seeing the mixed art styles (from the games, shows, etc) offers a new perspective to my dream as a kid.

Ambience, Sound Art, 2019

This piece combines my own field recordings of a trickling water fountain, the sound of people talking and the bathroom. I created a composite of these three different sounds in order to express how I feel when walking through the Student Union during peak times. As I walk through, it is often so busy that the sound of people talking or using the facilities begins to merge and melt into one.

Raining Cows, Digital Image, 2019

This is a digital composite images using various asian drinks and cows. A large part of my childhood involved asian drinks such as aloe, soy milk and banana milk. While normal milk is seen as a basic necessity, to me, drinks made to be a different flavor are much better.

Milk carton, Digital Image, 2018

This is a glitched digital image of an art piece that I drew digitally. This was originally done for a class exercise, where we experimented glitching images using either Audacity or by editing the text itself.

Self-Portrait, Interactive Net Art, 2019

This interactive net art is a self-portrait of myself in the digital world. Each link is an ad that I have gotten suggested for me. Some ads are accurate while others are not but that is for the viewer to see.
