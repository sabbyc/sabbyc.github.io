Sabrina Chan

Portfolio Artist's statement Contact

Sabrina Chan was born in Berkeley, California in 1999. She currently attends San Jose State University in San Jose, California and is working towards receiving her BFA in Digital Media Art. While she places most of her focus and energy into her studies, she also takes commissions on the side for various individuals. She offers sketches and colored illustrations. Chan has always been curious about learning how technology and human interaction go hand in hand and would like to gain more experience in UI and UX design.
